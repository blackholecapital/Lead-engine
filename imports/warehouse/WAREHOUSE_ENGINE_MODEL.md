# Factory XYZ Warehouse Engine Model

Tracer AI selects, scores, tags, and stages resources.

Runtime-C consumes staged resources and assembles software.

Flow:
request
-> Tracer AI resource selection
-> temp resource bundle
-> Runtime-C orchestration
-> generated artifact
-> review center
-> promoted product

Purpose:
This is the reusable engine for future product development, upgrades, demos, and client builds.
